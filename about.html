About

Ola IITM BS Students and Synapse'ians!

Welcome to the Official Website of Synapse Society. Synapse is the official Social Studies, Language Study & Cultural Study Society of IITM BS Degree.

The Society collectively focuses on History, Geography, Political Studies, Languages, Cultural-heritage, Philosophy and Humanities.

If you think you've an interest in developing your Socio-Linguistic knowledge and Social skills, making yourself a better representative, enhancing and inculcating the significance of our culture, understand the realm of Languages, learn languages, practicalize Geography and Political Studies, Synapse Society is always here for you.

Together,

Let's take a step forward and make Synapse a healthy family.

Goals

Synapse Society has a diverse range of goals.

Our Primary focus is on enhancing and engaging the Socio-Linguistic Knowledge and Social Skills among our members.

Apart from this, secondarily, Synapse Society aims for spreading Language & Cultural Awareness, increasing Historical Awareness and appreciation, explore geography and it's diverse set of knowledge, exhibit and publish the cross disciplinary connections between all the Socio-Linguistic fields, providing mentorship and networking opportunities to embed perfection, advocate for education and preservation of culture & heritage, enhance public development and social support & making the person a better ciitzen.

Together, we'll make a difference.
